Hello, I am Andrey Mihaylov Petrov, a student at the language high school "Acad. Lyudmil Stoyanov". My interests from an early age have been related to computers and chess. At the age of 6, I won my first chess medal and since then I have spent my time on the computer. If you want to learn more about my chess achievements, please scroll down.

Here I won 3rd place in the National Individual First Rapid Chess Championship. The competition was held in Sofia and lasted two days.

In 2024, at the National Team Championship with the team "Viktori", I won first place in classical chess. The competition was held in Borovets and the battle was so fierce that only half a point separated us from 2nd and 3rd place. The final round was filled with nerves, anxiety, and joy; in the end, everything ended happily.

Here, I managed to finish in 5th place in a major international classical chess tournament. The tournament included Greeks, Macedonians, and others. I scored 6/4.5, with my only loss coming from the tournament winner.
